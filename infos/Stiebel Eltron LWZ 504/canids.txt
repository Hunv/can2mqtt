180 = LWZ (Main)
301 = Heat Circuit 1
302 = Heat Circuit 2
6A0 = Display at the LWZ (Terminal Address 3)
6A1 = Display at the LWZ (Terminal Address 4)
6BC = Display at the LWZ, but "core" for Time and Operating status (When used with Terminal Address 3, if it may change)
